# **App Name**: Local Leap

## Core Features:

- Review Funneling: Direct users to an internal feedback form if they rate 1-4 stars and redirect 5-star reviews to the Google Maps page to maximize positive feedback. Filtered reviews are available as a tool for the company to engage and address concerns.
- GMB Optimization: Optimize Google My Business profiles by uploading, editing, and organizing photos, managing categories and tags, controlling geolocation, and configuring strategic keywords.
- Automated Posting: Schedule and automatically publish promotions, events, and news to Google Business Profile listings to maintain an active and relevant presence.
- Performance Dashboard: Display real-time data on profile visits, calls, route requests, and website clicks, along with monthly performance evolution, for clear and informed decision-making.
- Landing Page Builder: Enable businesses to design customized landing pages with logos, colors, photos, descriptions, links, and contact options via a visual editor, with templates that are optimized for conversion.
- Client Management: Enable the creation, modification, suspension, and removal of business accounts. Allow global configuration of settings, including language preferences, and support white-label options, pricing, and subscription plans.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated blue (#4285F4) to evoke trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Light blue (#E3F2FD) for a clean and calming feel.
- Accent color: Orange (#FFA726) to highlight calls to action and important information.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' for a modern, readable, and neutral feel.
- Use a consistent set of professional icons to represent different features and data points. Favor clear and recognizable symbols for ease of understanding.
- Employ a clean, intuitive layout that facilitates easy navigation and information discovery. Ensure a responsive design that adapts seamlessly to various screen sizes and devices.
- Incorporate subtle animations to enhance user experience and provide feedback on interactions, such as loading indicators or transitions between views. Use animations sparingly to maintain a professional feel.
